## Business Problem
Ebay leadership observed strong Q4 revenue but suspected profit erosion due to heavy holiday discounting and uneven regional performance.

## Objective
Analyze transactional, product, and customer data to:
- Identify revenue peaks
- Evaluate category profitability
- Measure regional performance
- Track customer retention

## Tools Used
- SQL (joins, aggregations, profit calculations)
- Power BI (interactive dashboard)
- Excel (data validation & cleaning)

## Key Insights
- December revenue peaked at $6.52M
- Clothing category led both volume and profitability
- West region achieved highest AOV
- 2,052 customers made repeat purchases across Q4

## Business Impact
Provided leadership with data-backed insights to optimize discount strategy, prioritize high-margin categories, and improve customer retention.
